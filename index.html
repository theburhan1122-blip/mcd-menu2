Top 8 McDonald's Items You Must Try

Big Mac®

The Big Mac needs no introduction. Two beef patties, special sauce, lettuce, cheese, pickles, and onions on a sesame seed bun make this an all-time favorite. It's messy, flavorful, and totally worth it.

Quarter Pounder® with Cheese

Made with fresh beef cooked right when you order, this burger delivers juicy flavor in every bite. The melted cheese and slivered onions complete this satisfying classic.

Chicken McNuggets®

Crispy on the outside, tender on the inside. These bite-sized pieces of white meat chicken pair perfectly with any dipping sauce. Great for sharing or keeping all to yourself.

Egg McMuffin®

The breakfast sandwich that started it all. A freshly cracked egg, Canadian bacon, and American cheese on a toasted English muffin. Simple, delicious, and filling.

Spicy McCrispy™

If you love heat, this one's for you. A crispy chicken fillet with a spicy pepper sauce that brings just the right kick. Perfectly crunchy and packed with flavor.

McFlurry® with OREO® Cookies

Creamy vanilla soft serve mixed with crushed OREO cookie pieces. It's the perfect treat after any meal or as a standalone snack on a hot day.

World Famous Fries®

Golden, crispy, and lightly salted. These fries are iconic for a reason. Best enjoyed fresh and hot straight from the fryer.

McCafé® Iced Coffee

Smooth, refreshing, and available in different flavors. Whether you like it plain or with caramel, this drink gives you the caffeine boost you need without the heaviness of hot coffee.

Frequently Asked Questions

What time does McDonald's stop serving breakfast?

Most locations stop breakfast around 10:30 or 11:00 a.m.

Does McDonald's have all-day breakfast?

Not everywhere. Check your local restaurant for availability.

Are McDonald's fries vegetarian?

In the U.S., they contain natural beef flavor with milk derivatives.

What's inside a Big Mac?

Two beef patties, special sauce, lettuce, cheese, pickles, onions, and a three-part bun.

Can I customize my order?

Yes, you can add or remove items through the app or at the counter.

Are Chicken McNuggets made with real chicken?

Yes, they're made with 100% white meat chicken.

Does McDonald's offer gluten-free options?

No certified gluten-free items are available in the U.S. due to cross-contact risks.

What dipping sauces come with McNuggets?

Options include BBQ, Sweet 'N Sour, Honey Mustard, Ranch, and more.

Can I get free refills on drinks?

Many locations offer free refills for dine-in fountain drinks.

Where can I find nutrition info?

Check the McDonald's app or official website for full details.
